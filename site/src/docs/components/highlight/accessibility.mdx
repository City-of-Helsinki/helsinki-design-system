## Accessibility
- Both the Quote and Highlight elements are non-interactable and therefore do not have any special keyboard functionality.

## Responsiveness
- Both the Quote and Highlight components have three (3) size variants; Large (L), Medium (M) and Small (S)
- Sizing variants differ in typography size and inner spacing.

### Pay attention to
- Both the Quote and Highlight elements uses theme for custom styling.
