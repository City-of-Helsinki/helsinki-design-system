### Principles

- Highlights are designed to capture users' attention. ** Therefore, they should be used sparingly. **
- Use the prop `type="quote" ` to transform the highlight text into a quote with an optional reference, though we strongly recomend adding a reference to a quote
- Pay attention to colours when using custom text or accent line color
- Quote and Highlight adds vertical margin before and after actual content
