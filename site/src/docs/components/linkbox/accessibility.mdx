## Accessibility

### Pay attention to
- Linkbox must have the link as its last element (in the DOM tree). This way assistive technologies will eventually find the Linkbox link.
- The colour contrast between the Linkbox and its background must comply with <ExternalLink href="https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21/#contrast-minimum">WCAG AA Level contrast ratios</ExternalLink>. If needed, you may use a Linkbox border to increase the contrast to its background.
