# Commonly known Helsinki cookies

<StatusLabel type="error">Draft</StatusLabel>

<LeadParagraph>
  For data security reasons, all cookies are handled in subdomain level and each service is responsible for their own cookies. However, there are some cookies that are used in most services so it is beneficial to use them the same way in all services.
</LeadParagraph>

## Key principles of using common cookies

- The cookies are handled in subdomain level and each service is responsible for their cookies
- Known cookies for common services such as Helsinki Profile or Matomo should be used and described the same way in all services.
- You can either use all or a part of the common cookies.
- Note that some of the services (e.g., Matomo) may require multiple common cookies for it to work.

## Common cookies to be used in \*.hel.fi sites

The following is a list of known cookies for common services like Matomo or Tunnistamo.
